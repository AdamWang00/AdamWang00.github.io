My Recent Projects

1st@Brown

Android/iOS mobile app that allows first-year students to meet others who share common interests. Features Google sign-in, content moderation, and global and private chatrooms powered by socket.io.

Coordinator

Web app that finds optimal meeting times for a group of people based on each person’s availability. Subgroups can be formed using a variation of k-means clustering. The interface is accessible to users with disabilities.

Circles

Won the Wolfram Award at Hack@Brown 2021. Android/iOS social media app that allows users to make plans and share memories with their friend circles. Users can control content visibility to protect their privacy.

Noteflow

Web app for composing AI-generated melodies using Magenta.js. The music is rendered in staff notation, which users can listen to, adjust for key and tempo, save to their accounts, and share with others.

Bear Essentials

Android/iOS mobile app allowing Brown University students to view dining schedules, laundry room statuses, and campus maps. Features include subscription-based notifications and stored user preferences.

AMDUG Website

Modern responsive website for Brown’s Applied Math Departmental Undergraduate Group. Features an event blog and contact form maintained by admin accounts.
